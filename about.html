Who I Am

I'm Sayantan — a Product Development Manager who's spent the last three-plus years at the intersection of technology, business strategy, and user experience. I grew up in Chattogram, Bangladesh and completed my BBA in Management at North South University, Dhaka.

My journey started in coordination and communications, which gave me an early appreciation for stakeholder dynamics and operational discipline. When I moved into product management at Barikoi Technologies, I discovered what I was truly built for: defining problems, shaping solutions, and measuring outcomes that actually matter.

How I Work

I'm a data-first, empathy-driven PM. I don't build products on intuition alone — I track North Star Metrics, run A/B tests, and conduct competitive deep dives before committing to direction. But I also believe that numbers never tell the full story; the best insights come from sitting with users and watching them struggle (or delight).

I've led the end-to-end lifecycle of multiple SaaS products across fintech verification, HR management, and last-mile delivery logistics — and I've integrated AI (LLMs, RAG) into production environments that serve real users at real scale.

Beyond Product

When I'm not mapping user journeys, you'll find me on the chess board — studying positions, calculating lines, and thinking three moves ahead. It's the same discipline that makes me a better PM: anticipate, adapt, execute.
